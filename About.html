About Rich Motors

Trusted vehicle sourcing, service, and support for Rwanda.

Since 2015, our Kigali team has helped drivers, families, and businesses import, purchase, and rent with clarity and confidence.

Our story

We started with a promise: no surprises, only reliable vehicles.

Rich Motors launched in Kigali to give customers a better experience when importing, buying, or renting vehicles. We focus on verified cars, transparent pricing, and a team that supports you every step of the way.

  • Verified vehicles with full inspection reports
  • Transparent pricing in RWF with clear payment terms
  • Local support from our Kicukiro team
  • Flexible sales, rental, and delivery options
